DevHousing

Web App for DevMountain's housing




Client: DevMountain Group Project

My Role: Front-End Focused Developer

View Git Repository View Online


A housing portal for DevMountain, streamlining the process of students entering and exiting the program's housing as well as providing good user experience during the program. Made in a group, my part of the group was focused on the HTML and CSS with some backend.

On the student side, the students can submit work orders and see vital information about what is going on.

The Admins can assign the students to the housing units in three seperate DevMountain locations and edit FAQ, Work Orders and other vital information.
