I have done tasks with a variety of the front end functionality and look of the site. Making them friendly for all screen sizes, from mobile to tablet to desktop.

I have also been tasked to improve and maintain the search functionality of the site using ElasticSearch. Refactoring code for a newer version to bring more accurate and faster searching as well as auto-complete and suggestion among other new features and functionality for search.
